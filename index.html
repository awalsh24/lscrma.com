Who We Are

Purpose-Built for
This Moment

With Veeva CRM entering Stability Mode and Salesforce Life Sciences Cloud emerging as the clear successor, life sciences companies face one of the most complex CRM transitions in decades.

Life Sciences CRM Advisors was founded specifically to guide organizations through this transformation. We bring deep industry expertise, a structured methodology, and — critically — no vendor bias. We work with both Veeva and Salesforce so our recommendations are always in your interest.

We don't implement software. We ensure you choose the right platform, build the right plan, and set up your implementation partner for success.

How We Work

A Structured Path from
Assessment to Execution

Every engagement follows a rigorous, phase-based methodology. Each phase has defined deliverables, clear milestones, and executive checkpoints — so you always know where you stand and what comes next.

drag or use arrows to explore

01

Project Framing

Plan and organize for success.

Draft the Project Charter covering scope, objectives, deliverables, milestones, and project structure. Establish logistics and operating model from day one.

02

Assessment

Understand the current state.

Discover and document the full CRM ecosystem — software, data, partners, and processes. Map pain points and identify gaps against current and future needs.

03

Evolution Vision

Define where you're going.

Drive consensus on the future-state CRM vision — priorities, principles, capabilities, and goals. Build the value story that leadership can rally around.

04

Platform Decision

Choose the right CRM.

Lead a structured, quantitative evaluation of Vault CRM and Salesforce Life Sciences Cloud. Deliver RFI/RFP templates, capability worksheets, and an executive briefing with a clear recommendation.

05

CRM Evolution Roadmap

Build the high-level plan.

Develop the strategic migration roadmap covering timing, sequencing, workstreams, dependencies, and directional cost estimates. Deliver a Go/No-Go executive briefing.

06

Migration Governance

Oversee execution.

Provide ongoing program governance and oversight — ensuring milestones are met, risks are managed, and business value is achieved throughout the implementation.
